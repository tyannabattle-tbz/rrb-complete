## 1. Introduction

The Rockin' Rockin' Boogie platform is a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to preserve and continue the musical legacy of Seabrun Candy Hunter. Built by Canryn Production, the platform serves as a living archive, broadcasting hub, community center, and autonomous management system. It embodies the mission of Sweet Miracles Foundation — "A Voice for the Voiceless" — providing the community with access to essential tools during crises, enabling them to produce their own media, broadcast as they choose, and access information and communication.

This manual covers everything an end user needs to navigate, interact with, and benefit from the platform.

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Accessing the Platform

Open your web browser and navigate to the platform URL. The site is a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning it works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. For the best experience on mobile, use the "Add to Home Screen" option in your browser to install it as a standalone app.

### 2.2 Creating an Account

Click the **Sign In** button in the navigation bar. You will be redirected to the Manus OAuth portal. After authenticating, you will be returned to the platform with a session cookie that keeps you logged in. No separate registration is required — your Manus account serves as your identity across the platform.

### 2.3 Navigation Structure

The platform is organized into two primary sections reflecting the legacy mission:

| Section | Purpose | Key Pages |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Legacy Restored** | Historical content, verified documentation, the music | The Music, Proof Vault, Timeline, Verified Collaborators |
| **Legacy Continues** | Active operations, broadcasting, community | Radio, Podcasts, Meditation Hub, Studio Suite, Divisions |

The main navigation bar provides access to all top-level sections. On mobile devices, a hamburger menu reveals the full navigation tree.

## 3. Core Features

### 3.1 The Music

Navigate to **The Music** to explore Seabrun Candy Hunter's catalog. This page presents verified songs with BMI registration data, including "Rockin' Rockin' Boogie" and "Let's Work Together." Each entry displays the song title, artist credits, publisher information (Payten Music), and links to external verification sources.

### 3.2 Proof Vault

The **Proof Vault** is the platform's evidence repository — a curated collection of verified documents, screenshots, and external references that establish Seabrun Candy Hunter's authorship and legacy. Items are organized by category:

- **BMI Songview** — Screenshots from BMI's public database confirming song registrations
- **Publisher Credits** — Discogs and other sources confirming Payten Music publisher credit
- **Copyright Office** — U.S. Copyright Office registration records
- **Streaming Platforms** — Presence verification across Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, etc.
- **Legal Documents** — Court records, correspondence, and other legal evidence

Each proof item can be expanded to view full details, screenshots, and external verification links.

### 3.3 Radio Broadcasting

| Channel | Content | Frequency |

| Channel 1 | Rockin' Rockin' Boogie Main | 24/7 |
| Channel 2 | Healing Frequencies | 24/7 |

Use the global audio player at the bottom of the screen to switch channels, adjust volume, and control playback. The player persists across page navigation.

### 3.4 Podcasts

The **Podcasts** section provides video-integrated podcast episodes with a dedicated viewing screen. Features include interactive elements, bot AI assistance, and a call-in feature for live feedback and interaction. Episodes are organized by series and can be browsed, searched, and played directly.

### 3.5 Meditation Hub

Access the **Meditation Hub** for healing frequency sessions based on Solfeggio frequencies (174 Hz, 285 Hz, 396 Hz, 417 Hz, 432 Hz, 528 Hz, 639 Hz, 741 Hz, 852 Hz, 963 Hz). Select a frequency, set a duration, and begin your session. The platform provides ambient visualizations and timer controls.

### 3.6 Solbones Dice Game

**Solbones 4+3+2** is a sacred math dice game supporting 1–9 players with QUMUS AI opponents. Access it from the Entertainment section. The game features custom dice skins, Solfeggio frequency audio playback during gameplay, and a scoring system based on sacred mathematics.

### 3.7 Sweet Miracles Foundation

The **Sweet Miracles** section provides information about the 501(c)(3) / 508(c) nonprofit foundation. Users can make one-time or recurring donations to support legacy recovery efforts. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. To donate:

1. Navigate to **Sweet Miracles**
2. Choose a donation tier or enter a custom amount
3. Click **Donate** — you will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout
4. After payment, you will be returned to a confirmation page

## 4. QUMUS Autonomous System

### 4.1 What is QUMUS?

QUMUS is the central autonomous brain that controls all platform systems. It operates at approximately 90% autonomy with 10% human interaction, maintaining human override capabilities for critical decisions. As a user, you benefit from QUMUS through:

- Automated content scheduling and rotation
- Intelligent recommendations
- Real-time system health monitoring
- Autonomous error detection and recovery

### 4.3 Royalty Audit & MusicBrainz

The Royalty Audit Policy (Policy #12) monitors all revenue streams across 6 platforms (BMI, Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, SoundExchange, and more). It includes **MusicBrainz integration** that cross-references song registrations and collaborator credits against the open music database. Access the Royalty Audit dashboard at **Admin > QUMUS > Royalty Audit** to view sources, discrepancies, audit reports, and MusicBrainz cross-references. For a complete guide on setting up revenue streams from your airwaves, see the **Royalty Collection Guide** in the docs folder.

### 4.4 Community Engagement

The Community Engagement Policy (Policy #13) tracks listener interactions across all 9 channels (radio, podcast, forum, donation, Solbones, meditation, social, HybridCast, merchandise). It monitors engagement scores, donation patterns, campaign effectiveness, and member tiers (new, active, engaged, champion, ambassador). Access the dashboard at **Admin > QUMUS > Community Engagement**.

### 4.5 AI Content Generation

The AI Content Generation Policy (Policy #14) uses the built-in LLM to auto-generate show descriptions, social media posts, broadcast schedules, and promotional content for all 7 channels. It includes a template system with configurable prompts, an approval workflow (auto-approve above 85% confidence, escalate below), and a scheduler for recurring generation. Access the dashboard at **Admin > QUMUS > AI Content** to manage templates, review pending content, approve/reject/publish, and view generation reports.

### 4.6 CSV Payout Data Import

The Royalty Audit dashboard now includes a **CSV Import** tab that accepts payout reports from DistroKid, TuneCore, CD Baby, Spotify for Artists, and Apple Music Analytics. The system auto-detects column names (title/song/track, artist, streams/plays, earnings/revenue, platform/store, period/date) and cross-references actual payouts against expected rates to detect discrepancies automatically.

## 5. HybridCast Emergency Broadcast

- Cross-platform notifications
- Accessibility-first design for impaired community members

Access HybridCast from the Emergency Broadcast section in the navigation.
