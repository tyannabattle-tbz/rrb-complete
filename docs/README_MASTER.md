## What Is This?

The Rockin' Rockin' Boogie (RRB) Ecosystem is a comprehensive digital platform preserving the musical legacy of Seabrun Candy Hunter, powered by the QUMUS autonomous orchestration brain. It manages broadcasting, evidence preservation, royalty auditing, community engagement, AI content generation, emergency communications, and nonprofit operations — all under 90% autonomous control with 10% human override.
